What to wear
Whatever you want! But here are some suggestions:
• Simple clothing works best
• Minimal jewelry and accessories
• Wear what fits your personality

Where to shoot
Ironically, your own home or backyard can be one of the best places to shoot--it has an emotional value that most other places don't.
Some other suggestions:
    • Rockford Park
    • Valley Garden Park
    • Rockwoood Park
    • Winterthur
    • Hagley
    • Longwood Gardens
